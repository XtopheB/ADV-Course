Takeaways

  • Remove unnecessary ink
  • Too many colors (Color is not decoration)
  • Simplify background
  • Use grey
  • Use small multiples
  • Refine, refine
  • Identify a question
  • Direct labeling
  • Use analytics!

Tell the story, not the data set!
